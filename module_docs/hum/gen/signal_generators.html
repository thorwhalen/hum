Returns a generator of lists, with each consisting of n repetitions of a random word for alphabet, with n being determined by a normal distribution with mean N and variance N * spread_pct >>> gen = gen_words(N=3, alphabet=(‘foo’,), spread_pct=0) >>> assert next(gen) == [‘foo’, ‘foo’, ‘foo’]
