hum.examples.tagged_intervals.tagged_intervals_gen(tag_model=None, items_per_tag=None, start_bt_s=0)[source]

Generates a sequence of tagged intervals in the form of dicts of the form {“tag”: str, “bt”: numerical, “tt”: numerical} where bt is the lower bound of the interval and tt is the upper bound of the interval.

Parameters:

  • tag_model – A dict whose keys are the tags and values are the generative model for that tag.

  • n_items – Number of items to be generated for each tag. If None, one item for each tag will be generated.

  • start_bt_s – The start time in seconds.

Returns:

A generator yielding dicts representing tagged intervals.
